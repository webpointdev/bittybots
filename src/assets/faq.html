Polygon

What is Polygon?

Polygon is Layer 2 scaling solution for Ethereum and a fantastic choice for our NFT. Polygon is very fast and scalable, enabling gas fee of ~$0.01 or less, which is crucial for this project as we do 10X more on the contract compared to other projects.

How do I get started?

Get a Metamask chrome extension.

How do I connect to the Polygon Chain?

If the Button on the Navbar doesn't help you add the Polygon Chain automatically for you, then you might need to follow these steps to add the Polygon network to your Metamask wallet.

How do I get MATIC or Polygon ETH?

MATIC is the coin required to pay for gas fee and also buy and combine BittyBots. You can watch a Youtube Tutorial here.

It's a little complicated but please bear with us. First, you need to understand that ETH and MATIC can be on different chains. In order to purchase BittyBots on this site, you need to have MATIC on the Polygon chain. Once your MATIC is only Polygon, the gas fee becomes negligible.

Method 1 (two gas fees)

  • 1. Get MATIC on the Ethereum mainnet using Uniswap or some other services like Coinbase. This might require a gas fee.
  • 2. Go to the Official Polygon Wallet and use "Move Fund from Ethereum to Polygon" to move your MATIC from Ethereum to Polygon. This will also require a one-time gas fee.
Method 2 (one gas fee)
  • 1. Bridge your ETH to Polygon using the Official Polygon Wallet. This will require a gas fee.
  • 2. Swap the ETH with MATIC on a service like QuickSwap. You will need a little bit of MATIC to pay for the gas fee for this swap and you can get it for free from this Faucet (0.003 MATIC per day).
Method 3 (free)
  • 1. If you're only looking to use your free combine, you can get some MATIC to perform the operation for free from this Faucet (0.003 MATIC per day).

My wallet is connected but I don't see my bots.

As this site fires a lot of web3 requests, sometimes the RPC node will be congested. You can either check back later or change your RPC node to a different one.

What's an NFT?

An NFT, or “non-fungible token”, is a new way of collecting art. Compared to traditional art, it uses crypto technology and allows everyone to publicly see each others' collection. Each NFT has a programmable smart contract behind it that allows creators to do creative things such as airdropping, breeding, burning, or generating new tokens.

Combine

How do I "combine" my BittyBots?

You can head over to the Combine page (when launched) and choose between 2 to 5 of the BittyBots you own, at a time. You will be able to customize a new BittyBot by selecting your favorite parts from the bots you have selected. This action cannot be reverted and the selected BittyBots will be burned.

Can I combine multiple times?

Combining selected bots is a one-time irreversible operation. The BittyBots used to combine will be burned to mint the new bot. You can combine bots up to 3 times.

Can I combine into a BittyBot that already exists?

All BittyBots are unique. So you cannot create a BittyBot that already exists. The combination used for detecting duplication is as follows: Helmet, Face, Body, Arm, Engine.

What does it cost to combine?

The first combine is free of charge. The second combine will cost 100 MATIC and the final combine will cost 200 MATIC. The second and third combine are only there for people who want to build the strongest bot.
